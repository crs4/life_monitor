Life Monitor API

Workflow sustainability service

Life Monitor aims to facilitate the sharing, execution and monitoring of workflow tests over time, ensuring that deviations from the workflow's correct operation are detected and communicated to the workflow authors so that they might be solved, thus extending the useful life of the workflow.

Life Monitor is being developed as part of the EOSC-Life project.
